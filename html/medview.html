Medview

MedView is a healthcare SaaS platform concept that envisions a user-friendly solution for efficient record-keeping and clinical operations, showcasing the potential for innovation in the industry.

Project Duration

1 day

Categories

SaaS, Healthcare, Healthtech

Tool[s] Used

Figma

The Homepage

The homepage serves as the gateway to MedView, offering a clean and intuitive interface. It introduces users to the platform's core features and sets the tone for a seamless healthcare management experience.

Sign in Page

The sign in page provides a secure entry point for authorized users. It incorporates user authentication measures, ensuring data privacy and confidentiality. The design focuses on simplicity and accessibility.

Dashboard

MedView's dashboard is the central hub for users, presenting a comprehensive overview of essential healthcare data. It streamlines access to patient records, appointments, and administrative tools. The design prioritizes user experience, promoting efficiency in clinical operations.
