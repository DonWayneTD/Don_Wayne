Handworka

Handworka is a Nigerian startup dedicated to empowering individuals with technical and vocational skills for a promising future. It operates as an online platform, allowing users to create accounts, explore diverse courses, manage their learning journeys through personalized dashboards and an active whatsapp community .

Handworka's Objective

The primary objective of this project is to provide users with
access to vocational and technical skills through Handworka’s
online platform. Simultaneously, we aim to foster a
dynamic and engaging learning experience by maintaining
an active WhatsApp community.

My Roles

Facilitated communication between the Growth Team and the Design Team.

Collaborated with the Growth Team to review designs such as social media design flyers.

Addressed user needs and concerns through Handworka's active WhatsApp community.

Worked alongside the lead designer in the Design Team to enhance the user experience.

Conducted user interviews with people from the community to gain valuable insights into their experience using the platform and the community.

Handworka's Design Process and Evolution

My part in the project began with redesigning a HubSpot landing page, serving as the primary user acquisition gateway. Unlike the initial design,
my redesign was aimed at transforming this page into an effective onboarding tool. It incorporated brand elements, highlighted the value proposition,
promoted the skills users would acquire, and optimized the data collection process. These enhancements were pivotal in integrating new users into
Handworka's thriving WhatsApp community.

Initial Design

Lacked an onboarding process

Didn’t elaborate on the unique value proposition

Didn’t have the needed look and feel of Handworka

Redesigned Version

Introduced an onboarding process

Elaborated on the unique value proposition of handworka

Improved the look and feel of the page

Handworka's Design Process and Evolution Continued

To attract users, I was tasked with designing a dedicated ebook landing page, offering a free ebook in exchange for joining the WhatsApp community.
Check it out here.

I was tasked with crafting an events landing page, where Handworka hosted skilled experts to educate and inform users on various vocational skills
and courses.

Recognizing the need for consolidation, we transitioned to a WordPress Learning Management System (LMS). Customized to reflect Handworka's
branding, this streamlined our operations by providing a single platform for all pages

The WordPress LMS now allows users to enroll in a diverse range of vocational courses. Users can easily purchase and participate in courses. Skilled
individuals also have the opportunity to become instructors, contributing to the growth and diversity of the platform.

Challenges

Initially, user acquisition was scattered across various landing pages.

Design limitations on Hubspot’s CMS

Ensuring effective communication between the Growth Team and Design Team.

Gathering valuable insights into user experiences and pain points within the WhatsApp community.

Driving engagements on the community

Solutions

Consolidated multiple landing pages into a single WordPress LMS platform.

Simplified the process of joining the WhatsApp community by offering a free ebook.

Conducted user interviews to address pain points and improve the WhatsApp community's user experience.

Results

Integrated landing pages significantly improved the efficiency of user acquisition

The WhatsApp community thrived, fostering active engagement and knowledge sharing

User insights contributed to improved user experiences within the community

Tools Used

HubSpot was utilized for CRM (Customer Relationship Management), data analysis, and the initial design of CMS (Content Management System)
landing pages.

Figma was employed for designing cards, creating a design system, developing brand guidelines, designing personas, and user interview questions.

Wordpress LMS was utilized as the main platform for Handworka’s online learning platform

Canva was a major tool utilized by the growth team for designing flyers and promotional materials

Key Takeaways

I initially joined Handworka with the expectation of contributing to community engagement. However, I soon discovered a more profound purpose
- to empower users with valuable vocational and technical skills through their online platform.

My time at Handworka has been an incredible learning experience. I gained insights into user engagement, platform development, and bridging the
gap between design and growth teams. I've honed my skills in user interviews and witnessed the transformative power of a supportive community.

As I continue my journey with Handworka, I'm excited to contribute further to their mission. I aim to explore innovative ways to enhance user
experiences, facilitate learning, and ensure that the Handworka community thrives.
